Pair 2 — Wings for flyingPure horizontal at ±90°. The longest pair — maximum span, maximum reach. The cross-chain movement made visible.
Pair 3 — Wings covering the feetAngled ±142° from vertical. Mirrors the top pair, grounding the mark. Reverence, balance, completeness.
Central diamond — the divine coreWhite-hot point at origin. Every wing radiates from here. The source of fire, the source of data.

Usage Rules
✓ Do
  • Use the six-wing sigil as the primary Seraph mark
  • Always pair the sigil with the Cormorant Garamond wordmark
  • Keep crimson as a precision accent — never flood it
  • Maintain dark backgrounds — Seraph is dark-mode-first
  • Use "ELYON LABS" as wordmark only — no icon for the company
  • Use violet subtly — cosmic depth behind the primary crimson
  • Always write SERAPH in uppercase in brand contexts
✗ Don't
  • Don't place the sigil on photographic or textured backgrounds
  • Don't scale the sigil below 16×16px — use the wordmark alone instead
  • Don't use the sigil mark for Seraph Labs company materials
  • Don't rotate or skew the sigil from its vertical axis
  • Don't use crimson as a background fill — it's an accent only
  • Don't mix Cormorant Garamond with any other serif typeface
  • Don't write "seraph" lowercase — it's always Seraph or SERAPH
