II.
The ManifestoOn Cross-Chain Clarity

The chain boundary is a fiction.

Value has learned to cross. It moves between ledgers through bridges and messaging layers, and each layer pretends the others do not exist. Ethereum tells you a transaction left. Arbitrum tells you one arrived. No one tells you what happened in between — and when something fails, you are left reading four explorers at once, guessing.

Seraph is built on a simpler premise: every cross-chain transaction has exactly one story, and that story should be legible from a single surface. Source, bridge, destination. Hash, relay, hash. State, state, state. Nothing omitted. Nothing embellished.

We watch eight protocols across fourteen chains so you do not have to. When something moves, we see it. When it finishes, we know. When it stalls, we can tell you where.

— Seraph Labs · April MMXXVI
